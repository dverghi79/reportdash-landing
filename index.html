The problem

Reporting is your least billable work and your most client-visible deliverable.

01

You manage 5 client accounts across Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and TikTok. Every month, you log into each platform, screenshot the analytics, copy the numbers into a Google Slides template you built yourself, and add some commentary. It takes 3-4 hours per client. For 5 clients, that is an entire Friday or Saturday you do not bill for.

02

Tools like Reportei and SproutSocial solve this for agencies with multiple team members and $500/mo budgets. You are a solo freelancer. You do not need a client portal, a white-label dashboard, and 40 integrations. You need a clean PDF that makes your client feel informed and keeps them renewing. That tool does not exist yet.

03

Your clients judge your value by what they see — and most of what they see is your monthly report. If it looks like you spent 20 minutes on it, that is what they think your work is worth. If it looks like a strategic brief from someone who runs their business like an agency, they renew. The platforms give you the data. You just spend hours formatting it.

How it works

Connect your client accounts. Reports generate themselves.

Step 1

Connect all your client accounts in one place

ReportDash connects to Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and TikTok via API. You link each client's accounts once — no manual logging in, no screenshot harvesting, no copy-pasting from Business Manager.

Step 2

Monthly reports generate automatically

On the first of every month, ReportDash generates a branded PDF report for each client account — reach, engagement, follower growth, top posts, and a plain-English performance summary. Each report is ready before your client asks for it.

Step 3

Send in one click, look like an agency

Each report arrives with your name, your branding, and a clean layout that takes 60 seconds to read. Your client gets a deliverable that reflects professional management — and you spent zero Sunday hours building it.

Built for

Reclaim 3-4 hours per client per month.

The average solo social media freelancer spends 3-4 hours per client on manual monthly reporting. With 5 clients, that is 15-20 hours a month — the equivalent of a billing week spent on admin. ReportDash cuts that to zero.

Reports that make you look like an agency, at freelance economics.

Agency-grade reporting tools cost $200-500/mo and require a team to manage. ReportDash is built for a solo freelancer who wants their client-facing work to look like it came from a 5-person shop — without the 5-person budget.
